Which countries are eligible for the TikTok Creator Rewards Program?

As of 2026, the program is confirmed available in the following countries:

  • United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia
  • Germany, France, Italy, Spain
  • Japan, South Korea
  • Brazil, Mexico
  • Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates
  • Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines

If your country is not on this list — and many are not (most of Eastern Europe, Africa, South Asia, and Central Asia are excluded) — the Creator Rewards Program option will simply not appear in your Creator Tools, regardless of your follower count or view numbers.

⚠
VPN does not work
TikTok cross-checks your SIM card country and the IP address used when you registered your account. Changing your location via VPN after registration does not change the account's registered location. The Creator Rewards Program will not appear for ineligible accounts regardless of what network you connect through.

What are the full eligibility requirements for the Creator Rewards Program?

  • At least 10,000 followers on the account
  • At least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days (not lifetime — rolling 30-day window)
  • Account must be at least 30 days old
  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Personal account (Business accounts are not eligible)
  • Account in good standing — no recent policy violations or strikes
  • Account registered in an eligible country
  • Videos must be at least 1 minute long to qualify for rewards
→
The 30-day view requirement
The 100,000-view threshold is the one most creators get stuck on. This is not lifetime views — it is 100,000 views accumulated in the 30 days immediately before you apply. If your account is growing but has not had a strong viral month yet, you may need to wait for the right window before applying.
