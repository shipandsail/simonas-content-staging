What is the main difference between Tikomate and HardLaunch?

The primary difference lies in the infrastructure model and pricing accessibility. HardLaunch hosts real Android phones on physical server racks, charging a steep monthly subscription. Tikomate provides pre-verified accounts, proxy setup workflows, and scroll-behavior simulations to warm accounts safely on your own hardware, with no monthly subscription floor.

  • Tikomate: Human scroll gesture simulations, verified US accounts from $25, and proxy setup blueprints.
  • HardLaunch: Remote hosted mobile hardware, automated content variations, and high-frequency posting schedules.
