Do you need followers to be a TikTok Shop affiliate?

Yes, but the bar is much lower than Creator Rewards. Unlike the Creator Rewards Program (which requires 10,000 followers and 100,000 views), TikTok Shop Affiliate requires just 1,000 followers to apply. Accounts between 1,000 and 5,000 followers enter a 30-day Pilot Program with limited access, up to 5 promo videos and 3 LIVEs per week, before unlocking full affiliate capabilities at 5,000 followers.

This makes TikTok Shop Affiliate far more accessible than Creator Rewards. The content-driven discovery model means a new account can start driving sales and building commission income well before reaching Creator Rewards thresholds.

What is the TikTok Shop fee structure for 2026?

Understanding the seller side helps affiliates negotiate better commission rates. Because the seller's total platform cost is 6%, many US brands offer affiliates commission rates between 5% and 20% depending on product margin. High-margin categories (beauty, supplements, digital products) consistently offer the most generous affiliate rates.
