  • Creator Rewards Program is US-first: TikTok's main payment program for views is only available in select markets. The US market pays the highest RPM — typically $0.40–$2.50 per 1,000 views depending on niche, compared to fractions of that in most other eligible countries.

What are the ways to make money on TikTok outside the US?

1. Creator Rewards Program (requires US account)

The Creator Rewards Program is TikTok's primary pay-per-view monetization layer. Eligible creators earn based on video views, with payouts influenced by originality, engagement, and audience geography. Videos must be at least 1 minute long and meet a baseline threshold of 1,000 plays to qualify for payment.

For creators outside the US, accessing Creator Rewards requires a US-registered TikTok account. A VPN alone does not work — TikTok cross-checks the SIM card country, IP address, and payment information used at account creation to determine eligibility. An account registered in a non-eligible country will not see the Creator Rewards section in the app, regardless of follower count.

  • Minimum requirements: 10,000 followers, 100,000 views in the past 30 days, account 30 days old, 18+ years old, personal (not business) account.
  • US RPM by niche: Finance and business ($0.80–$2.50), education ($0.50–$1.50), entertainment ($0.20–$0.60).
  • Videos must be 1 minute or longer — under 60-second content does not qualify for Creator Rewards payments.

2. TikTok Shop Affiliate (requires US account)

TikTok Shop affiliate is the most lucrative income stream for many creators in 2026. You add products from TikTok's US marketplace to your videos or live streams, and earn a commission (typically 5–20%) on every sale that comes through your content. Unlike brand deals, there is no minimum follower count required to apply as a Shop affiliate — the barrier is simply having a US-registered account.

The US TikTok Shop marketplace has millions of products across beauty, fashion, electronics, home goods, and supplements — all with affiliate programs open to creators. A well-placed product recommendation in a video that reaches the right audience can generate passive income for weeks after posting.

  • No follower minimum to join TikTok Shop affiliate — the US account requirement is the only gate for international creators.
  • Commission rates range from 5–20% per sale, set by each merchant individually.
  • Products can be featured in posted videos, LIVE streams, or TikTok's product showcase on your profile.
  • Payouts are processed through TikTok's standard withdrawal system once you reach the payment threshold.

3. Brand deals and sponsored content (available anywhere)

Brand sponsorships are negotiated directly between creators and brands — TikTok's account location does not affect your eligibility. If you have an engaged audience of any meaningful size, brands will pay for sponsored content. Rates scale with follower count and engagement rate, not with account country.

However, US-registered accounts attract higher-value brand deals on average, because brands paying for sponsored content want their products in front of US consumers. International creators posting to non-US audiences typically attract lower-value brand partnerships from brands targeting those regions.

  • Brand deal rates at 50K followers: typically $200–$500 per post for general niches, higher for beauty, tech, and finance.
  • Creators can list themselves on platforms like TikTok's Creator Marketplace to attract inbound brand inquiries.
  • Unlike Creator Rewards, brand deals do not require account eligibility — just audience size and engagement.

4. TikTok LIVE gifts (partially available outside US)

TikTok LIVE allows viewers to send virtual gifts that convert to diamonds and then to real money. LIVE gifting is available in more countries than Creator Rewards — you do not necessarily need a US account to receive gifts. However, the conversion rates and maximum withdrawal amounts differ by account region, and US accounts consistently offer better earning conditions.

Requirements for LIVE access: 1,000 followers minimum, 18 years old, account in good standing. Once live, creators can earn from viewers in any country who choose to send gifts during the broadcast.
