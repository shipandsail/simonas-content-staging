TokPortal Pricing (2026): Every Plan, Credit Cost & What It Actually Runs You

Updated July 12, 2026 · 6 min read
TL;DR
  • TokPortal uses a credit-based subscription model: Starter $49/mo (54 credits), Creator $95/mo (112 credits), Marketer $195/mo (260 credits), Farmer $595/mo (992 credits). Source: tokportal.com/pricing.
  • Account setup alone costs 25 credits, meaning a single Starter-plan month (54 credits) only covers roughly 2 new accounts plus a handful of uploads before you're buying more credits.
  • TokPortal is not listed on G2 as of this writing. On Trustpilot it holds a 3.4/5 TrustScore from 15 reviews, with pricing and cancellation friction as the most common complaint themes. Source: trustpilot.com/review/tokportal.com.

TokPortal doesn't publish a flat per-account price, its pricing runs on a credit system where every action (account setup, uploads, warming, editing) draws down a shared credit pool. That makes the real monthly cost hard to estimate from the pricing page alone. Here's what each plan actually includes, straight from TokPortal's own site, plus what independent reviewers say about whether it delivers.

What are TokPortal's actual subscription prices?

TokPortal runs four monthly subscription tiers, priced on a declining per-credit rate as you move up tiers. All figures below are pulled directly from TokPortal's pricing page.

Plan Monthly Price Credits Included Cost per Credit
Starter $49/mo 54 credits $0.90
Creator $95/mo 112 credits $0.85
Marketer $195/mo 260 credits $0.75
Farmer ("Best value") $595/mo 992 credits $0.60

Source: tokportal.com/pricing, fetched July 12, 2026. Marketer tier unlocks full analytics (engagement rate, watch time, impressions) and enriched comment scraping; Starter and Creator get basic analytics (views, likes, followers) only. Farmer supports up to 20 accounts with warming and 100 uploads. All plans include a 15% discount if billed yearly and can be cancelled anytime, per TokPortal.

What does a TokPortal credit actually buy?

Because the plans are priced in credits rather than seats or accounts, the real cost depends entirely on what you're doing with them. Per TokPortal's pricing page:

  • Account setup: 25 credits per account
  • Video upload: 2 credits per video
  • Niche warming: 7 credits
  • Deep Instagram warming: 40 credits
  • Video editing: 3 credits
  • Sound-volume control: 1 credit

Does TokPortal also sell credits without a subscription?

Yes. TokPortal offers pay-as-you-go credit packs, valid for 60 days from purchase, for buyers who don't want a recurring plan:

Pack Size Price Cost per Credit
5 credits $9 $1.80
250 credits $250 $1.00
500 credits $450 $0.90

Source: tokportal.com/pricing. Custom pricing is available for 1,000+ credits/month with dedicated support, per TokPortal.

⚠
Pay-as-you-go is the most expensive route
The smallest pay-as-you-go pack ($9 for 5 credits) works out to double the per-credit rate of the Farmer subscription ($1.80 vs $0.60). One-off buyers pay a real premium versus committing to a monthly plan.

What do real TokPortal customers say about the price?

TokPortal holds a 3.4 out of 5 TrustScore on Trustpilot from 15 reviews as of this writing (46% five-star, 7% four-star, 20% three-star, 27% one-star). It does not currently have a listing on G2. Pricing is one of the recurring themes in lower-rated reviews:

  • A 3-star reviewer from the Netherlands wrote simply: "too expensive for what you get." TokPortal's team replied that it isn't trying to be the cheapest option, positioning itself against "cheap distribution" that runs on automation rather than real people and devices.
  • A 1-star reviewer from Malaysia cited "expensive pricing" alongside complaints about UI changes and unclear cancellation flow. TokPortal's reply disputed the account-access claims and offered to investigate directly.
  • Higher-rated reviewers (5-star, from the UK, Ireland, Turkey and Poland) praised the team's responsiveness and reliability rather than commenting on price specifically.

All quotes and ratings from trustpilot.com/review/tokportal.com, accessed July 12, 2026.
