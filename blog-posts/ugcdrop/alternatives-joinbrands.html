JoinBrands is one of the largest creator marketplaces available, 4M+ creators, unlimited campaigns, and support for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Amazon content in one dashboard. Why do people look for alternatives to JoinBrands?

JoinBrands works well for brands with the team bandwidth to manage creator campaigns at scale. But several structural issues consistently push brands to look elsewhere:

  • Layered cost structure: UGC videos start at $25 per creator, but the Free tier adds a 15% platform fee, making the real minimum $28.75/video before any subscription. Adding the Start-up plan ($99/month) for meaningful outreach features pushes the effective monthly commitment significantly higher.
  • Creator management overhead: JoinBrands requires briefing creators, reviewing applications, managing communication, requesting revisions, and tracking deliverables. For small teams, this is a meaningful time investment per campaign.
  • Revision limits: The Free plan allows only 1 revision per video. Getting to a final asset requires careful upfront briefing, and mistakes cost you a revision round.
  • Full feature access requires a paid plan: The Free tier doesn't include access to the 4M+ creator pool, daily outreach, analytics, or email/phone/Spark Code retrieval. Meaningful campaigns require the Start-up plan minimum ($99/month).
