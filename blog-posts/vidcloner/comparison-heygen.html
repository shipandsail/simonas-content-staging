HeyGen offers 300 to 1,500 stock presenters depending on the subscription level. 2. Recreating Virals: Copy-Link Remakes vs. Slide Scripting

Replicating trending content is the fastest route to viral views. VidCloner has a built-in "Remake" feature: you copy the link of any viral TikTok or Reel, paste it into the dashboard, select your avatar, and VidCloner swaps the presenter while preserving the pacing and background.
