What is Delulu Cam?

Delulu Cam describes itself as a real-time identity layer for your camera, not a filter or preset. Instead of just editing your existing face on screen, it replaces it live with either an uploaded image or an avatar you build yourself. The pitch is explicitly about live use, streaming, calls, recording, and social content, rather than editing a video after the fact.

How does the Creation Studio work?

Delulu Cam's Creation Studio is where you build the avatar that replaces your face. You can upload images to define an identity, or build one from scratch, and switch between saved avatars instantly once they're set up.

Based on Delulu Cam's own product description, the workflow is:
1
Upload or build an identity
Upload a reference image, or build an avatar from scratch in the Creation Studio, choosing from styles like Pixar-style, anime, realistic, or clay.
2
Set Delulu Cam as your virtual camera
In whatever app you're using, Zoom, Meet, Teams, Discord, OBS, and similar, select Delulu Cam as the camera source instead of your device's built-in camera.
3
Go live
The identity swap runs in real time on the live feed, no per-clip editing or rendering wait, according to the product's own claims.
