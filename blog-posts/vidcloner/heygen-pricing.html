HeyGen is one of the most searched AI avatar video tools, and its pricing structure has changed over time as the product has matured. This page reflects HeyGen's published pricing page as verified in July 2026, since older pricing summaries circulating elsewhere (including some third-party listings) no longer match what HeyGen currently charges.

How much does HeyGen cost?

As of July 2026, HeyGen's published pricing runs: Free ($0), Creator ($29/month), Pro ($49/month), Business ($149/month plus $20 per additional seat), and a custom-priced Enterprise tier. Annual billing lowers the effective monthly rate slightly on Creator and Pro.

Voice cloning is not a Pro-only feature
HeyGen's current pricing page confirms unlimited voice cloning is included starting on the $29/month Creator plan, it is not held back for a higher tier. If you've seen older content (including some of our own earlier drafts) claiming otherwise, that reflects a prior pricing structure, not what HeyGen charges today.

What's the difference between HeyGen's Creator and Pro plans?

Creator ($29/month) includes 600 credits per month, video length up to 30 minutes, and 1080p export. Pro ($49/month) increases that to 1,000 credits per month, adds 4K export, and processes videos faster. Both plans include unlimited voice cloning and 175+ language support.

  • Creator ($29/mo): 600 credits, 1080p export, best for individual creators posting regularly
  • Pro ($49/mo): 1,000 credits, 4K export, faster rendering, the better fit for higher-volume or higher-resolution needs
  • Both tiers: Unlimited voice cloning, unlimited photo avatars, 175+ supported languages

What do the Business and Enterprise tiers add?

HeyGen's Business plan is $149/month plus $20 per additional seat, and adds videos up to 60 minutes, 5 custom digital twins, team collaboration tools, and SCORM export for training content. Enterprise pricing is custom and adds no duration limits, faster processing, and multi-workspace control, contact HeyGen's sales team for an exact quote.

Is there a lower-cost option if you only need video cloning?

If your specific goal is cloning viral videos, mapping your own avatar to an existing video's motions and timing, rather than HeyGen's broader avatar-video suite, VidCloner's Starter plan starts at $19/month for 5 video credits and basic avatar uploads, a lower entry price than HeyGen's $29/month Creator plan.

The two tools aren't solving identical problems. HeyGen is a general-purpose AI avatar video generator, you write or feed in a script, and an avatar delivers it. VidCloner is built specifically around cloning: you paste the URL of an already-proven video, and the platform maps your avatar to that video's exact movements and timing. For creators whose main goal is replicating a viral format rather than scripting new avatar content from scratch, that narrower focus can mean a lower price for the specific feature they actually need.
