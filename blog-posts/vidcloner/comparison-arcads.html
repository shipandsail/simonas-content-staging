What is the main difference between VidCloner and Arcads?

The primary difference is how content is made. Arcads is a library of pre-made, synthetic AI characters: you write a script, and a generic stock presenter reads it. VidCloner is a viral video cloning engine: you paste the URL of any TikTok or short-form video, select your avatar, and the AI replaces the original actor with your face, matching the same motions and timing of the source clip.

Go Deeper: Side-by-Side Breakdown

1. Presenter Options: Your Avatar vs. Stock Characters

Arcads offers a catalog of pre-made AI actors. Because these actors are shared across thousands of Shopify brands, your ads will often feature the exact same virtual characters as your competitors. VidCloner bypasses this by using your avatar. You select your avatar, paste a viral video URL, and your face replaces the original actor, ensuring complete brand exclusivity without any filming.

2. Creative Workflow: Link Remakes vs. Text-to-Video Scripts

With Arcads, you must write scripts, select actors, and build scenes manually. VidCloner includes a one-click TikTok "Remake" feature: paste any public video URL, select your cloned avatar, and VidCloner swaps the presenter while matching the pacing, cuts, and vocal rhythm of the original viral video.

3. Pricing Economics: Transparent Self-Serve vs. Hidden Pricing

Arcads hides its pricing behind a signup wall, with subscriptions starting around $110/month for 10 finished video exports ($11/video). VidCloner is fully transparent: Starter tier is $19/month for 5 credits ($3.80/credit), Creator tier is $49/month for 15 credits ($3.27/credit), and Master tier is $59/month for 30 credits ($1.96/credit).
