Go Deeper: Side-by-Side Breakdown

1. Presenter Library: Realism vs Volume

Arcads is generally regarded as slightly more realistic in gesture and posture rendering. HeyGen counters with sheer volume: 300 presenters on its Creator plan, scaling to 1,500 on Pro, giving brands far more demographic variety to match a target audience.

2. Ad Tooling: Export-Only vs Competitor Intelligence

Arcads is a straightforward script-to-video exporter with no built-in ad research tools. HeyGen's Pro plan adds custom avatars, ad launcher integration, and competitor ad intelligence, features aimed at media buyers running paid campaigns rather than just producing raw video files.

3. Pricing: Hidden Signup Wall vs Published Credit Tiers

Arcads requires signing up before any pricing is shown, with estimates placing its Starter tier near $110/month for 10 exports. HeyGen publishes its Creator plan at $29/month and Pro at $49/month, making budget comparisons far easier upfront. Neither offers a way to put your own face in the video, both rely on a shared stock avatar catalog.
